YV Practice

Coffee Shop
Conversation

Practice simple present, daily routine verbs and questions with do and does.

💡 Remember: He / She / It + verb + S. For questions, use Do or Does.

Read and listen

Part 01
Lucas: Hi! Is this chair free?
Emma: Yes, it is. Please, sit down.
Lucas: Thank you. My name is Lucas.
Emma: Nice to meet you, Lucas. I’m Emma.
Lucas: Do you come here every day?
Emma: Yes, I do. I love this coffee shop.
Lucas: What do you usually order?
Emma: I usually drink a cappuccino and eat a muffin.
Lucas: Sounds good. I drink black coffee.
Emma: Do you work nearby?
Lucas: Yes, I do. I work in an office across the street.
Emma: What time do you start work?
Lucas: I start at 8 a.m.
Emma: That’s early!
Lucas: Yes, but I finish at 4 p.m.
Emma: Lucky you! I finish at 6 p.m.
